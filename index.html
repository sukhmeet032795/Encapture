How Does Reiki work?

The Life Force flows within us, within the physical body through pathways called chakras and meridians. It also flows around us in a field of energy known as Aura. Life Force nourishes the organs and the cells of the body, supporting them in their vital functions.

When this flow of Life force is disrupted, it interrupts the functions of one or more of the organs and tissues of the physical body. Life force corresponds to our thoughts and feelings. It becomes disrupted when we accept, consciously or unconsciously, negative thoughts or feelings about ourselves or others.

Reiki heals by flowing through the affected parts of the energy field and charging them with positive energy. It raises the vibrations of the energy field in and around the physical body where negative thoughts and feelings are attached. This breaks the negative energy. Reiki clears, heals and balances the energy pathways, thus allowing the life force to flow in a healthy and natural way.

Card Reading Courses

  • Tarot Card Reading

    Tarot Card reading is the practice of using Tarot Cards to gain insight into the past, present and future by formulating a question or some questions, then drawing and interpreting cards.

    The Tarot deck is made up of the Minor Arcana and the Major Arcana. The Cards depict stories and are drawn according to the spreads for life situations, questions. A trained Tarot Card Reader can help you understand the stories behind these cards which act as mediums to unravel the messages from the angels and the Universe.

  • Lenormand Fortune Card Reading

    Lenormand Cards are a deck of 36 images of different things that you would come across in day to day life and nature such as house, child or a fox.

    They allow us to read the future, give literal interpretations, and look at upto 36 different areas of a seeker’s life. In a flash, one can get a snapshot of someone’s love, career and spirituality.
